Who will win the race of AI? The US or China? Everyone wants a simple answer, but the truth is more interesting. First, look at the United States. It still leads in frontier models and raw computing power. Now look at China. It ships more AI papers and patents than any other nation, and its open models are catching up fast. Here is the number that actually matters: 90 percent of the most advanced chips come from one small island between them. So the real winner? Whoever controls the supply chain.
